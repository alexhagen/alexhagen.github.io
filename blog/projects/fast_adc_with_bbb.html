Code (software and firmware) at github.com/alexhagen/fast_adc_yun

I’ve created a fast analog to digital conversion system using only an Arduino Yun (and an external computer to process data). The system can sample the Arduino Yun’s ADC at 8-bit at greater than 30 kSps and then send those samples out via UDP at the same rate. With a fast enough external computer (most modern computers would be), you can then catch most of those samples without error and plot them, or log them.
